奥山 幸

Sachi OKUYAMA

東京、ウィーンにて寿司を握り、ヒルトンホテルにて腕を振るう。2001年の春、自らのレストランをオープン。2004年にはブダペストの50のベストレストランの1つに選出。小さな島で生まれ、子供の頃から魚介になれ親しむ。当店の魚は日本から選んできたものを自らの手で捌いてる。

The master chef OKUYAMA　began his career in Tokyo. Later, he was invited to Vienna, where he spent 5 years practicing the art of making sushi. In recognition of his expertise and competence, he was invited to the Hilton Hotel. In the spring of 2001, He opened his own restaurant in 2004. The restaurant was rightly chosen as one of the 50 best restaurants in Budapest. He was born in a small island, so he has known fish well since he was a child, he had the opportunity to study different fish species up close. He brings the fish by plane from abroad, and he processes them himself.
